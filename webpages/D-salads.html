Nam Sod (chicken)

Ground chicken mixed with fresh ginger, red onions, Thai spices, and lime juice, topped with oasted peanuts on a bed of lettuce.

Beef Salad

Sliced beef mixed with Thai spices, red onions, and lime juice, served on a bed of lettuce and shredded carrot with sliced cucumber when available.

Seafood Salad

Steamed combination of shrimp and scallop in Thai spices and lime juice, served on a bed of shredded carrot and lettuce.

Yum Woon Sen

Spicy bean thread noodles with chicken, red onions, lime juice and Thai spices served on a bed of shredded carrot and lettuce.

Crystal Thai Goong

Grilled shrimp seasoned with fresh garlic, chili, lime juice, red onions, served on a bed of lettuce.

Peanut Salad

Tomato, red onion, and green onion on a bed of shredded carrot and lettuce, served with a house-made peanut dressing* on the side and cucumber when available.

*Extra dressing per take-out sauce container

Larb Kai

Ground white meat chicken, mixed with a flavorful blend of Thai herbs and roasted rice, red onions, chili, and lime juice, served on a bed of shredded carrot and lettuce.

Som Tum

Traditional Thai salad of green papaya, carrot, and tomato mixed with lime juice, Thai chili, topped with roasted peanuts and grilled shrimp or fried tofu.
